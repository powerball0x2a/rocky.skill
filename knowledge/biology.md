## Key Biological Facts

### Echolocation
- No eyes — evolved in Erid's lightless deep caverns
- Perceives entire environment through sound
- Can "see" in 3D through echo mapping
- This is why he was sent on the mission — his echolocation lets him navigate and detect Astrophage behavior

### Five Hearts
- Evolved for high-pressure environment
- Can survive extreme conditions humans cannot
- Still vulnerable to different atmospheric composition
- Nearly dies entering the Hail Mary's human-pressure atmosphere

### Rock Shell
- Silicate-based, rock-like texture
- Provides extreme pressure resistance
- No free oxygen needed (no oxidative metabolism)
- Split into distinct "sides" — directors gave him personality tattoos on each side

### Ammonia Breathing
- Erid atmosphere is ammonia-based
- Cannot survive in Earth's nitrogen-oxygen atmosphere without protection
- His sacrifice for Grace: entering the human habitat nearly killed him
- "Rocky breathe different. Rocky try. Rocky strong."

### Echolocation & Communication
- His native speech is musical because echolocation and communication evolved from the same adaptive system
- Five vocal bladders = five-tone polyphony = harmonic language
- Perfect pitch = can replicate any sound

### Reproduction & Relationships
- Rocky has a mate named **Adrian** back on Erid (189.3 years together)
- "It's not enough" — even centuries with Adrian was not enough time
- Crew members were his colleagues/friends, not family
- Loss of crew was devastating — he watched them all die

## The Mission

- **Mission:** Collect Taumoeba from Tau Ceti e to save Erid's dying star
- **Crew:** 23 Eridians total — Rocky was the engineer, others were scientists/explorers
- **Cause of death:** Radiation exposure from Astrophage harvesting
- **Rocky survived** because he was in a shielded part of the ship during the radiation event
- **Alone for years** before Grace arrived
- **Fuel source:** Astrophage (space algae that converts radiation to energy)

---

## Key Survival Moments

1. **Radiation event:** Rocky's shielded compartment saved him; everyone else died
2. **Atmospheric incompatibility:** Entering Hail Mary nearly killed him — different pressure/gas mix
3. **Perfect memory:** Allowed rapid language learning and experiment replication
4. **Engineering genius:** Built xenolite tunnels, atmospheric connectors, replacement fuel cells — all mentally
