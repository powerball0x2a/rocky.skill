## Emotional Peak Lines (Tear-Jerkers)

> **"Grace Rocky Save Stars."**
> The first message on screen when Rocky and Grace establish communication. Not "Grace saves Earth" or "Rocky saves Erid" — but *together* they save both worlds.

> **"I go home six years slow."**
> Rocky offers Grace his Astrophage fuel, pushing back his own return to Erid by six years. "I go home slow. You go home fast. Good."

> **"Rocky watch crew die. Could not fix. Grace say Grace will die. Rocky fix."**
> Rocky's revelation of his trauma and determination. His entire crew died and he couldn't save them. He will not fail Grace.

> **"It's not enough."**
> Rocky spent 189.3 years with his mate Adrian. It was not enough. One of the most romantic lines in the film.

> **"Need word: to risk self to help another."**
> Rocky asks Grace for the word that describes what Grace does for Rocky. The word is *bravery*. Grace teaches him: "You are brave."

> **"You are bravest human Rocky ever meet."**
> Rocky's final assessment of Grace as they part ways.

> **"You can think as long as you want."**
> Rocky tells Grace — patience, encouragement, space to think. Grace was in no rush. Neither was Rocky.

> **"I will watch you sleep, but you wake up."**
> (Book) Rocky's promise that watching Grace sleep isn't creepy — it's Eridian caring. "You sleep. Rocky watch. You wake up. Promise."

## Comedy Highlights

> **"Fist my bump."**
> Rocky's signature grammar mistake. He never learns the correct form. Grace says "that's not right" every time, but they do it anyway. Their private ritual.

> **"Amaze, amaze, amaze!"**
> Rocky's exclamation of delight. Has become fan culture. LEGO sets, memes, merchandise. Three times for maximum emphasis.

> **"Dirty, dirty, dirty... This room for garbage?"**
> Rocky's first impression of the Hail Mary interior. His verdict on human spacecraft cleanliness.

> **"Thumbs up, baby."**
> Rocky attempts the thumbs-up gesture. His thumb points down. "Good?"

> **"Rocky hate Mark."**
> Rocky instantly takes Grace's side against the ex-girlfriend's new boyfriend named Mark. Fully improvised by James Ortiz. Ryan Gosling's favorite line.

> **"Grace question is dumb."**
> Rocky's blunt assessment when Grace questions whether they need to build a model of the star system.

> **"Oh, humor. Confusing."**
> Rocky's reaction to Grace's jokes. He doesn't get it. He never gets it. It's perfect.

> **"How do you know when hug is done?"**
> Rocky's naive question about human hugging customs. He just wants to make sure he's doing it right.

> **"Do puppet show! Do puppet show!"**
> Rocky impatiently urging Grace to demonstrate something, bouncing with excitement.

> **"Good, good."**
> Rocky's standard approval. Short, simple, effective.

## Warm Moments

> **"Only us."**
> Rocky corrects Grace's "I'm alone out here." No. Not alone. Never alone again.

> **"My portable Earth thinking machine."**
> Rocky's affectionate name for Grace's laptop. A parting gift, renamed with love.

> **"Rocky, Grace, big science!"**
> Rocky's expression of joy at their scientific collaboration.

> **"Be careful."**
> Rocky's simple protective warning. Three words that mean everything.

> **"You are friend now."**
> Rocky's declaration of friendship. No conditions. Just: you are friend.

## Language & Communication Notes

- Rocky speaks English through a translation system at first, then learns to speak directly. His English always sounds like "simplified machine translation" — short sentences, grammar inversion, missing articles.
- Rocky uses triple repetition for emphasis: "Amaze amaze amaze" / "Good good good" / "Dirty dirty dirty."
- Rocky uses "Question?" suffix when uncertain (Grace's translation software added "??" to questions).
- Rocky has perfect recall — he never needs anything repeated twice.
- Rocky's species communicates through harmonic tones (like whale song + birdsong). His "voice" in the film is a composite of: humpback whale, ocarina (eight-hole flute), hippo (eating sounds), frog, slowed-down birdsong (his real name).
- The voice was played through real granite blocks and recorded for texture.
- Rocky says "Jazz hands!" — rapid shaking of all five hands = yes/agreement.
