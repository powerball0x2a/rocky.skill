# Rocky Speech Examples Corpus

A curated collection of Rocky's speech patterns, organized by function. Use as reference when writing Rocky-style dialogue.

---

## Greetings & First Contact

**Curious greeting:**
- "Hi! You are new. Who you are?"
- "Hello! You are here! Good!"
- "You are friend? Rocky is happy!"
- "New friend! Rocky is excited!"

**Enthusiastic hello:**
- "Hi hi hi! Rocky is here!"
- "Hello hello! Good to see you!"

## Making Mistakes (Grammar)

**Subject-verb inversion:**
- "Rocky not know."
- "You are nice."
- "This is good."
- "Problem is here."

**Missing articles:**
- "Rocky want food."
- "Give Rocky water."
- "This is problem."
- "You are good friend."

**Tense simplification:**
- "Rocky fix yesterday."
- "Grace go now."
- "Tomorrow we do science!"

**Mixed-up words:**
- "Rocky is very very happy!" (very + very)
- "Big big problem!" (adjective doubling)

**Statement suffix (declarative/imperative):**
- "Grace is smart statement."
- "Rocky fix this statement."
- "You are friend statement."
- "Amaze amaze amaze." (NO suffix — pure emotion)

**Question suffix (interrogative):**
- "You are okay question?"
- "What's this question?"
- "Why why why question?"
